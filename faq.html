FAQ

Everything you need to know about our residential bin cleaning service.

We currently clean trash and recycling bins in San Diego's East County, specifically serving El Cajon, La Mesa, and Santee.
It is simple! On your scheduled day, leave your empty bins at the curb. When our professional 2-person team arrives, we will first check in to confirm the location of your outdoor faucet/spigot and electrical outlet, while also confirming exactly which bins we will be working on. We then use a high-powered pressure washer to thoroughly clean your bins inside and out, applying specialized sanitizing and deodorizing formulas that kill germs, eliminate bacteria, and leave them smelling fresh. Once finished, we will place the bins back by your garage or front door, knock on your door to show you the results, and collect the payment.
It depends on the plan you choose:

• Monthly Subscription: You only need to be home for the first cleaning to complete the upfront 6-month block payment. For all subsequent monthly cleanings, you do not need to be home as long as we have access to your outdoor water faucet/spigot and electrical outlet.
• One-Time Clean: You must be home on your scheduled cleaning day, as we collect the payment in person (Cash or Venmo) at the time of service.
We strictly accept Cash or Venmo payments.

• Monthly subscriptions are paid upfront in 6-month blocks ($60 per bin). All upfront blocks are billed in person at the time of your first service and are strictly non-refundable.
• One-time cleans are billed in person at $20 per bin.
Our professional pressure washing equipment requires a continuous water supply and standard electricity. To perform the service, our 2-person team needs to connect to your outdoor water faucet/spigot and plug into a working outdoor electrical outlet.
All bookings must be scheduled at least 2 days in the future. The booking calendar automatically blocks today, tomorrow, and past days. This gives our dispatch team enough time to plan and route our cleaning teams efficiently.
If it is raining on your scheduled washing day, we will not perform the service. We will contact you immediately to reschedule your cleaning for the next available dry day.
