Pricing FAQs

How does payment work?

All payments are made in person at the time of your cleaning. We accept Cash or Venmo. Subscriptions are paid for 6 months upfront ($60 per bin) and are strictly non-refundable.

What if I have extra bins?

You can add additional trash, recycling, or yard waste bins to your plan. Each additional bin is charged at the same upfront rate of $60 in person for the 6-month period.

Do I need to be home for the wash?

Not necessarily, as long as we have access to your outdoor water faucet/spigot and a working electrical outlet. Just make sure they are turned on/accessible, and we will handle the rest on-site!
